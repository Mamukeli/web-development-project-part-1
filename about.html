About Us

Boost Force Mobile Car Wash is a Cape Town-based startup established in 2026 to disrupt the traditional queue-based automotive cleaning industry.

The business was created to provide customers with professional car cleaning services at their homes, workplaces, and convenient locations without wasting time in queues.


Mission Statement

To deliver premium vehicle care with zero time loss for the client.

Vision Statement

To be the premier on-demand detailing partner in the Western Cape.


Our Target Audience

Our primary target audience includes corporate professionals working in office complexes and residents in high-security estates who seek luxury convenience and professional vehicle care services.
